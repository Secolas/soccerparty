# Soccer Party — Screen-Recording Capture List

Exact in-game moments to record as **input frames / clips for Higgsfield**
(image-to-video). Pairs with `VIDEO_PROMO_AI_PROMPT.md` — the "Shot #" column
tells you which promo shot each capture feeds.

## How to capture (mobile portrait)

- **Record vertical (9:16)** on a phone or in a browser dev-tools mobile view
  (~390×844). This is a mobile-first PWA — capture it in portrait.
- Use the OS recorder (iOS Screen Recording / Android built-in) or, on desktop,
  Chrome DevTools device mode + a screen recorder (OBS / QuickTime / built-in).
- Record at the **highest resolution / 60fps** you can. Pixel art scales cleanly
  but you want crisp source.
- **Turn HAPTICS off** for recording (no effect on video) and **SOUND on** if
  you want to keep the chiptune reference for timing.
- Grab **5–10s** per clip and a few **held still frames** (pause on the peak
  moment) — Higgsfield needs a clean start frame, so a crisp paused frame is
  gold.
- Capture each moment **2–3 times**; you'll want options when picking start
  frames.

## Priority captures (the must-haves)

Ordered by importance. If you only grab the first 8, you can still cut the promo.

### 1. Logo / TAP TO START screen  → Shots 1 & 8
- **Setup:** cold-boot the game (hard refresh) so you get the boot gate.
- **Capture:** the stacked **SOCCER / PARTY** pixel logo with the spinning coin,
  before and at the "TAP TO START" prompt. Also grab the **loading bar** with the
  rolling coin + rotating TIP cards.
- **Frame you want:** logo centered, crowd twinkling, coin mid-spin.

### 2. Slingshot flick — the money shot  → Shot 2 (the hook)
- **Setup:** Exhibition, any bright pitch (GRASS or NEON reads best), PIECE: COIN.
- **Capture:** a full flick — drag **all the way back** so the aim arrow is long,
  hold a beat (show the green→red stamina color), then **release** and follow the
  coin streaking and **ricocheting off a wall** (sparks).
- **Frames you want:** (a) arrow fully stretched at max power, (b) the instant of
  release, (c) the wall-bounce spark.

### 3. GOAL celebration  → Shot 3
- **Setup:** line up an easy open-goal flick so you can score on demand.
- **Capture:** the coin hitting the net — **net bulge + ripple**, the **tifo
  banner** waving behind the goal, crowd swell, and the **scorebug** flipping the
  digit.
- **Frame you want:** peak net-bulge with the score just changed.

### 4. Pitch variety — NEON  → Shot 4A
- **Setup:** select the **NEON** board.
- **Capture:** a slow moment showing the glowing cyan/magenta grid, **flying
  drones**, and sweeping **light beams**. A pan across the board.

### 5. Pitch variety — AQUARIUM (signature)  → Shot 4B
- **Setup:** select the **AQUARIUM** board.
- **Capture:** the glass pitch with **deep-sea floor, coral, light columns, and
  rising bubbles** visible through the transparent surface. This is your most
  unique-looking pitch — get a clean hero frame.

### 6. Pitch variety — ICE  → Shot 4C
- **Setup:** select the **ICE** board.
- **Capture:** falling snow, **skaters gliding** around the rink, the red center
  line. Bonus: hit the ball into a wall to make nearby wildlife/skaters react.

### 7. STADIUM ROYALE map  → Shot 5
- **Setup:** ROYALE tab → open the map (`showRoyaleMap`).
- **Capture:** the **dotted path linking the 9 arena nodes**, the VS coin on the
  path, difficulty ◆ ratings, and the EASY/MEDIUM/HARD badge. Then enter a
  gimmicky arena — **PINBALL COBBLES** (bumpers/flippers) or **NEON GRID**
  (portals/lasers) show best on video.
- **Frames you want:** (a) the full map, (b) a boss arena with hazards active.

### 8. Coin economy  → Shot 7
- **Setup:** enable **COIN RUSH** (or play a Tournament/Royale where it's on).
- **Capture:** gold **coins spinning** on the board, a shot **rolling over them
  into the POT**, then **coins flying into the wallet** with the counter spinning
  up. Try to trigger an **ASSIST ×2** (deflect off your own player first) for the
  "ASSIST x2!" toast.

## Secondary captures (great B-roll / bonus shots)

### 9. VS intro cinematic  → bonus / alt opener
- **Setup:** start any match; the VS reveal plays automatically.
- **Capture:** the split-screen team reveal — **lightning bolt, embers, waving
  flag fan-heroes, the giant gold "VS"**, screen shake, clash. Great alternate
  cold-open.

### 10. Abilities firing  → Shot 6
- **Setup:** a match with abilities available (Tournament/Royale, or buy in the
  shop). Fire each on its own flick:
  - **CANNON** — power doubles, muzzle flash.
  - **CURVEBALL** — the shot bends in a glowing arc.
  - **CHIP** — the ball lofts airborne over defenders.
  - (Optional: **SNIPER** long aim line, **GUIDED/JOYSTICK** steer-after-release.)
- **Capture:** one clean clip per ability; keep the moment of activation in frame.

### 11. Setup phase / formation  → optional "strategy" beat
- **Capture:** dragging your outfield players into position before kickoff, and
  the FORMATION picker.

### 12. Team & kit selection  → optional "16 nations" beat
- **Capture:** the team carousel showing the **hand-pixeled national flags**
  (Brazil, Argentina, Croatia's checkerboard, Portugal's armillary sphere…) and
  the KIT style toggle (flag / jersey / away).

### 13. More pitches for a longer montage  → extends Shot 4
- Grab any of the remaining boards you like: **BEACH** (umbrellas, seagulls,
  ships, dog walker), **SAVANNA** (giraffe, elephant, antelopes, acacia),
  **STREET/BLACKOUT** (cars, street lamps), **CLAY/FIESTA** (dancers, doves,
  bunting), **COBBLE/LISBON** (yellow tram, pigeons).

### 14. Scorebug close-up  → cutaway / transition
- **Capture:** a tight hold on the **LED scorebug** — 7-segment clock, both
  pixel flags, the score digits, active-team highlight. Useful as a stinger.

### 15. Win screen / achievements  → outro support
- **Capture:** a **ROYALE CHAMPION** / cup-win screen, or the achievements list
  (FIRST WHISTLE, HAT-TRICK, JACKPOT, GOLDEN GOAL…). Good for the CTA lead-in.

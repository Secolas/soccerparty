## New stadium concepts (Season 2 brainstorm)

Each should introduce a genuinely new physics/hazard idea, with 3 signature
conditions (our per-stadium pattern).

### 🌊 UNDERWATER — "THE AQUARIUM" (medium, ~d2–3)
- **Buoyant ball** — floaty, slow-settling physics (drifts, gentle bounces)
- **Currents** — directional flow lanes that push the ball sideways
- **Shark/fish** — a swimmer that crosses the pitch and swats the ball
- *New mechanic:* a whole different "slow-motion water" ball feel.

### 🌙 MOON — "ZERO-G ARENA" (medium-hard, ~d3–4)
- **Low gravity** — big floaty bounces, ball keeps its speed longer
- **Meteor strikes** — telegraphed impacts that blast the ball away, leave craters
- **Tractor UFO** — a roaming saucer whose beam tugs the ball toward it
- *New mechanic:* low-gravity flight + a gravity-well puller.

### 🌋 VOLCANO — "MAGMA PIT" (hard, ~d4–5)
- **Lava cracks** — open/close on a timer; ball caught over one gets flung/bounced
- **Ember rain** — falling sparks that nudge the ball off-line
- **Rising tide** — a slow lava edge that shrinks the playable pitch mid-rally
- *New mechanic:* shrinking play area + timed floor hazards.

### ⚙️ FACTORY — "STEAMWORKS" (medium, ~d3)
- **Conveyor belts** — strips of floor that carry the ball sideways
- **Stamping pistons** — pistons that punch down and whack the ball
- **Spinning gears** — rotating obstacles that deflect it
- *New mechanic:* directional conveyor force along a lane.

Leaning: **Underwater** or **Moon** — both change how the *ball itself* feels,
which reads as a bigger "new stadium" moment than another obstacle course.
